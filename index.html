Michigan State Fishing Club

Our Mission

The purpose of this organization is to promote fishing opportunities for MSU students by providing a social network on campus for those who enjoy fishing. We strive to provide a welcoming community that caters to competitive and casual anglers by sponsoring tournaments (Like the MCBC) and arranging trips where members can become acquainted with types of fishing that they otherwise would not have been involved with.

Some of our biggest trips include steelhead fishing on the Pere Marquette, Ice fishing on Houghton Lake and the Saginaw Bay, and jigging for Whitefish on the Tawas Pier.

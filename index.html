Michigan State FIshing Club

About US

As the largest fishing-based organization on campus we pride ourselves on not only eductating fellow MSU students on different diciplines of fishing but providing a social network and events to get in touch and get out fishing.

Some of our largest trips include steelhead fishing on the Pere Marquette and ice fishing on Houghton Lake.

Meetings

We meet every tuesday at 7PM in room 158 of the Natural Resources Building. Come out and see what it is all about!

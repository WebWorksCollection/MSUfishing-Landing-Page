About US

As the largest fishing-based organization on campus, we pride ourselves on not only educating fellow MSU students on different fishing disciplines but providing a social network and events to get in touch and get out on the water. Whether it is bass, walleye, salmon or steelhead we cater to all fishing aspects

Trips

Some of our largest trips include steelhead fishing on the Pere Marquette and ice fishing on Houghton Lake.

Meetings

We meet every Tuesday at 7 PM in room 158 of the Natural Resources Building. Come out and see what it is all about!

Michigan State Fishing Club

Our Mission

The purpose of this organization is to promote angling opportunities for students by providing a social network for anglers at MSU (i.e. providing opportunities for anglers to meet, exchange information, and plan angling expeditions); organizing and participating in fishing tournaments; and organizing and participating in conservation, education and advocacy activities that promote angling.
